catmatch ai

Overview

CatMatch AI is a chatbot that recommends cat products based on behavior, health quirks, and owner preferences. It answers quick care questions while suggesting practical items that actually fit each cat’s personality.

Problem

Pet owners scroll endlessly through product pages and forums with no clear “this fits your cat” signal — too many choices, no guidance.

Solution

A structured chat flow that extracts traits like “picky eater” or “chaotic jumper”, then maps them to tagged products with mini explanations like “this slows down fast eaters”.
